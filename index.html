Yes! RealChoice™ Selling is a free service for home sellers. Our partner agents are local, full-service agents and experts in your specific markets. They have vast networks of resources including financing professionals, appraisers, inspectors, and contractors. They are also happy and willing to show you homes for your consideration and purchase.
Our service is fast, FREE, and incredibly easy to use. Here's how it works:
  • Submit a request online - it takes less than 2 minutes.
  • A Realtor.com Coordinator will call you to briefly discuss your specific needs and preferences.
  • You'll receive 3 to 5 personalized proposals within 24 hours.
  • Review and compare the proposals online and choose which agents to interview.
  • Finally, choose an agent to hire and get started!

If you are not 100% satisfied, there is no obligation, so you have nothing to lose and everything to gain!
A proposal is a personalized, detailed quote, specifically prepared for you by our agents in order to earn your business. They allow you to compare each agent to decide who offers you the best value.

Proposals for buyers and sellers are similar, but there are a few differences.

Seller proposals outline commission rates and services included (CMA, photography, virtual tours, staging, open houses, etc.), while buyer proposals include the buyer rebate where applicable. Both include agent profiles that outline reviews, stats, and more.

Your Realtor.com Coordinator can assist you in carefully reviewing and evaluating each proposal, should you have any questions or concerns.
We take your privacy very seriously. We will never sell your information or disclose it to any third parties without your approval. You may cancel your request at any time if you no longer need our services.
