The five steps.

A clear path, no improvised steps, no surprises. Here's what working with me actually looks like from the first call to closing day.

Get My Free Home Valuation
01

First call

Fill out the form or call me at 510-935-5701. We spend 15 minutes on the phone so I can understand your home, your timeline, and your goals. No pitch.

02

The walk-through

I meet you at your home and walk every room. I tell you honestly what helps the sale and what hurts it. Most agents flatter you to win the listing. I don't.

03

Your market analysis

Within 24 hours, you get a real comparative market analysis. Recent comps, active competition, recommended price, and a clear pricing strategy. You decide if and when to list.

04

Launch

Once you sign, your home is on the market in 48 to 72 hours. Professional photography, drone, video, MLS syndication, social campaigns, and a dedicated property page. All on a fixed timeline.

05

Negotiate and close

Offers come in. I walk you through each one so the decision is clear. Then I manage every inspection, contingency, and milestone through close. 30 to 45 days later, you have the wire.

Questions sellers ask.

Three things. How fast they respond to you during the sales process (a slow agent before you sign will be a slow agent after). Whether they'll tell you uncomfortable truths instead of just flattering you. And their track record in your specific price band and area. Everything else is noise.

I'm based in Irvine and work most densely across Orange County (Irvine, Newport Beach, Costa Mesa, Tustin, Lake Forest, Mission Viejo, Laguna Niguel) and the South Bay (Long Beach, Manhattan Beach, Hermosa Beach, Torrance). For sellers elsewhere in California, I work through a vetted referral network of agents I trust personally.

The five steps themselves are fairly standard across good agents. What separates agents isn't the steps, it's how many of them actually happen on time, how honestly you're treated at each stage, and how much work gets done when nobody's watching. If you want to see what that looks like in practice, the case files are worth reading.

In Orange County, a well-priced home typically goes under contract in 14 to 30 days, followed by 30 to 45 days of escrow. Six to ten weeks total from listing to keys. I build a specific timeline for your situation on the first call and update it at every milestone.
