Privacy Policy

Effective 11/10/2025 · drozq.com

Overview

This policy describes how drozq.com collects, uses, and protects information when you visit the site or submit a form. By using this site, you consent to the practices below.

What We Collect

Information you provide: name, email, phone, property details, and anything else you submit through a form.

Technical data: IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, and general location from server logs and analytics.

Marketing data: UTM parameters, campaign identifiers, and referral source so we can understand which channels generated the lead.

How We Use It

We use your information to follow up on your inquiry by email, SMS, or phone, to run and measure advertising, to improve the site, and to comply with legal requirements. We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and pixels from services like Meta and Google to recognize visitors, build custom audiences, and measure ad performance. Your visit may be used for retargeting. You can manage cookies in your browser and ad preferences on each platform.

How We Share It

We share limited data with service providers (hosting, CRM, email/SMS, analytics), advertising platforms, and, where relevant, brokerage partners handling your transaction. We may disclose information if required by law.

Your California Rights

California residents may request details about the personal information we collect, its sources and purpose, and may request deletion subject to legal exceptions. To make a request, contact us and mention that it is a “privacy request.”

Email and SMS

If you share your email or phone number, you agree we may contact you about your inquiry and related services. Unsubscribe from emails at any time using the link in the message. For SMS, reply STOP to opt out.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the site means you accept the updated policy.
