Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11/10/2025 • Site: drozq.com • Owner: Joshua Guerrero (d/b/a VIRELLO / associated projects)

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit drozq.com or submit your information through any form, funnel, or call-to-action hosted on this domain. This site is intended to function as a lead generation and nurturing environment for present and future offers, including (but not limited to) real estate services, brokerage services, and related advisory products.

By using this site, you consent to the practices described below.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Information you provide directly: name, email address, phone number, property details, business information, or any other data you submit through forms, calendars, or intake pages.
  • Lead intent data: the page you submitted the form from, the offer you expressed interest in, and timestamps.
  • Technical/usage data: IP address, device and browser type, pages viewed, referring/exit pages, and general location (city/region). This may be captured via standard server logs or analytics.
  • Marketing/attribution data: UTM parameters, ad campaign identifiers, and traffic source so we can understand which ads or channels generated the lead.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Lead follow-up: to contact you about the service, property, or offer you inquired about, via email, SMS, or phone.
  • Retargeting and remarketing: to show you ads across platforms (e.g. Meta, Google, other networks) based on your interactions with this site.
  • Funnel optimization: to analyze which pages, messages, or ads are performing and improve them.
  • Security and abuse prevention: to prevent spam and unauthorized activity.
  • Compliance: to maintain records required by law or legitimate business interests.

We do not sell your personal information in the sense commonly prohibited under modern privacy laws; however, we may share limited data with service providers and ad platforms to deliver the services described above.

4. Cookies, Pixels, and Similar Technologies

This site may use cookies, tracking pixels, and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize returning visitors.
  • Build custom audiences for ads.
  • Measure ad performance and conversions.

These tools may be provided by third parties (for example, Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, or other marketing/CRM platforms). These third parties may set their own cookies and process your data according to their own policies.

You can usually control cookies at the browser level. If you disable cookies, some lead tracking or personalization features may not function correctly.

5. Advertising and Retargeting

Because this site is designed to acquire customers profitably, we may use your visit to create or update advertising audiences. That means:

  • Your visit may be associated with an ad account (e.g. Meta, Google) for retargeting.
  • Your form submission may be used to create lookalike/similar audiences to reach people similar to you.
  • We may run sequential ads that speak to the specific stage you are in (cold → warm → hot).

If you do not wish to be retargeted, you can clear your cookies and/or use the “ad preferences” tools provided by the platform where you see the ad.

6. How We Share Information

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: hosting, email/SMS delivery, CRM, lead management, analytics.
  • Advertising platforms: to run and measure ad campaigns.
  • Business partners / future brokerage entity: if your inquiry relates to a real estate transaction or brokerage service that requires coordination.
  • Legal authorities: if required to comply with law, enforce our rights, or protect users.

We do not authorize third parties to use your data for their own independent marketing unless you have given that permission directly.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards appropriate to the size and current stage of this project to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. No system is perfectly secure, so you should only submit data you are comfortable sharing for business purposes.

8. Data Retention

Lead and contact data may be retained for as long as it is relevant to ongoing campaigns, follow-up, or required recordkeeping (for example, for real estate–related inquiries). Technical logs may be kept longer for security, fraud prevention, and analytics.

9. Your Rights (California / U.S.)

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to request:

  • What categories of personal information we collected about you.
  • What sources we collected it from.
  • The business or commercial purpose for collecting it.
  • That we delete certain information, subject to legal and business exceptions.

To make a request, contact us using the contact information made available on the main site and specify that it is a “privacy request.” We may need to verify your identity before responding.

10. Email and SMS Communications

If you provide your email or phone number, you agree we may contact you regarding the offer you requested, related services, and future opportunities. You can opt out of emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link. For SMS, reply with the opt-out keyword provided (commonly “STOP”).

11. Third-Party Links

This site may link to external sites (social platforms, schedulers, payment pages). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Review their policies separately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in how the site operates (for example, adding a new CRM, or scaling ad spend). When we do, we will update the effective date at the top. Continued use of the site after an update means you accept the updated policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise a privacy right, contact the site owner using the email or contact method listed on the homepage or primary sales page. Include enough detail to identify yourself and your request.
