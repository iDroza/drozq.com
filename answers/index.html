What if things are too risky?

Risk is often mistaken for Volatility. Things that are volatile oftentimes contain the most arbitrage because the masses only like to pursue the "sure things" the issue here is that you are compensated accordingly. At the core volatility is the arbitrage base upon which you play the game. The results are not guaranteed which is why they yield outsized returns. If you want mediocre returns please remain on the path of least resistance and the path of least stress.
